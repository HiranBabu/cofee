Coffee is produced from the seeds of a small red (sometimes yellow) fruit that grows on plants halfway in size between shrub and tree. The process that turns these seeds into beverage is a long and complex process, perhaps the most complex process associated with any major beverage. It is the cumulative quality of all of these creative contributions that together make the difference between a lackluster cup and a fine and distinctive one.
